Derelict

Derelict is a game me and a 5 of my peers made for our capstone project during my college course over the course of 3 months (3 weeks documentation - 10 weeks development). Derelict is a roguelike first person shooter with programable weapons which can be modified to create destructive weapon loadouts, all taken place in a rusted down spaceship with hostile robots to fights. Our inspiration is taken from Doom, Noita, and Quake, we thought a mix of these games and their features would make for an interesting game which to our knowlege hasnt been done before.

About this game

Noteable features:

  • 5 different weapons and 37 unique modifiers allowing players to build crazy loadouts for their weapons.
  • Dynamic weapon loadout system allowing modifiers to run in sequence, allowing certain modifiers to apply before others, giving players more control for how they want their weapon to function.
  • Modifiers range from changing your projectiles stats to changing what projectile you shoot, from passive healing modifiers to meta modifiers that dupilcate the action of a previous modifier.
  • Procedurally generated map for a unique map layout for each playthrough and level.
  • Steam audio integration for an realistic 3d sounding experience.
  • Intelegent AI Enemies that try to take cover when low on ammo or hold an angle for better shooting.
